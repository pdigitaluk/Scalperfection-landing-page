What is SMP?

A permanent, natural-looking solution to hair loss.

What it is
A permanent cosmetic treatment

Scalp micropigmentation places tiny deposits of pigment into the scalp to replicate the look of natural hair follicles — creating the appearance of a closely shaved head, or adding density to thinning hair.

01
How it works
Gradual, multi-session process

Treatment is built across multiple sessions, allowing pigment to settle naturally into the skin. This gradual approach is what makes the result look authentic rather than drawn on.

02
Who it's for
Men and women of all hair types

Suitable for pattern baldness, thinning hair, alopecia, scar coverage, and those unhappy with existing SMP from another provider.
